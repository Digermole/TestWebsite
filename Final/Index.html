About Me

Hello! My name is Shane Chavez. I graduated from CSUSB in the year 2023 with my bachelors degree in Computer Systems. My dream is to eventually make my own video game and present my vision to the world. For now though I seek to learn more about Web and Game Development. Some Hobbies I am passionate about are TTRPGS also known as Table Top RolePlaying Games, and Art. Every week I play in a few TTRPGS campaigns and I love making 3D art props for my campaigns or for campaigns I am apart of. I also love painting miniatures as it is very relaxing hobby to destress after a long day of coding.
